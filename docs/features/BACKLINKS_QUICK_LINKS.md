## Overview
New features have been added to make it easier to create backlinks and insert quick page links into your blog posts!

### 2. **Backlinks Generator Button**
Click the blue "Backlinks" button in the toolbar to automatically generate:
- Internal navigation links (Home, Blog, Categories, Tags, Search)
- Shareable links to the current post
- Ready-to-use markdown reference templates

### 3. **Backlinks & Quick Links Generator Panel**
In the admin dashboard (when creating or editing posts), there's now a beautiful generator panel that provides:

#### Backlinks Templates:
- **Simple Link** - Just the post link in markdown format
- **Full Section** - Complete backlinks section with navigation
- **References** - Pre-formatted citation examples

#### Quick Page Links:
One-click copy buttons for each page:
- Contact
- Blog  
- Newsletter
- About
- Full (all links combined)

## How to Use

### In the Editor Toolbar:
1. Place your cursor where you want to insert the link
2. Click the Quick Links dropdown or Backlinks button
3. Select your desired option
4. The formatted markdown will be inserted at your cursor position

### In the Generator Panel:
1. Scroll to the "Backlinks & Quick Links Generator" section (blue gradient background)
2. Select a template type (Simple, Full, or References)
3. Click "Copy" to copy to clipboard
4. Paste anywhere in your content
5. Or click any Quick Link button to copy that specific link

## Benefits

1. **Consistency** - All links use the same format and structure
2. **Speed** - No need to type out links manually
3. **Easy Navigation** - Readers can easily find related content
4. **SEO** - Internal linking helps with site navigation and SEO
5. **Copy & Paste** - Generate once, use everywhere

## Tips

- Use Quick Links at the end of your posts to guide readers
- Add References when mentioning other posts
- The Full Quick Links section works great as a post footer
- Backlinks help readers explore your site and improve engagement
